About Us

Nobole Events is an incredible and luxury event Management Company based in Montana Pretoria, A South African female owned business established in 2019. We are specialists in creating exclusive events to meet the most exclusive of tastes. Our event management services gives you exactly what you want bespoke events that creates everlasting memories because in your dreams, every detail matters. Nobole Wedding Planners offer our clients bespoke luxury weddings. Our luxury wedding planning division focuses on planning, design customization, and wedding planning event management service. We assist our clients in creating memorable, magical and meaningful wedding celebrations that exceed expectations.

Our personal approach ensures the luxury weddings we plan are truly a reflection of our clients as individuals and as couples. We try to bring in a touch of shared values and their own personal sense of style. We feel that great weddings are all about people and we always work with the guest luxury experience & finest detail in mind. Our luxury weddings are customized from conceptualization to execution. With an outstanding creative flair that is worked on from the beginning of our journey with the bride and groom taking their preferred style and putting it at the forefront of our designs. Our luxury wedding packages are fully coordinated with every last detail taken into consideration and all elements to make your special day a true celebration. We provide world class décor and floral designs, keeping up with the trends and ensuring that your luxury wedding is nothing short of spectacular
